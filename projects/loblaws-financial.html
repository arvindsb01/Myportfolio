Financial Modelling of Loblaws Limited

Duration: Feb 2024 - Apr 2024

Project Overview

This project involved building a comprehensive financial model for Loblaws Limited using various advanced financial techniques such as Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation, sensitivity analysis, and peer comparison to assess its overall financial health and investment potential. Through this exercise, I gained deeper insights into the company's financial performance and its standing in the retail industry.
