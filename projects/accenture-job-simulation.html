Accenture Virtual Job Simulation

📝 Project Overview 📝

This virtual job simulation was designed to simulate working as an Accenture consultant. The goal was to analyze business problems, identify solutions, and present findings to stakeholders. The experience provided me with valuable insights into consulting, problem-solving, and client communication.

🌟 Project Highlights 🌟

  • Business Analysis: Worked on real-world business problems related to strategy and consulting.
  • Skills Development: Developed strong problem-solving and communication skills essential for consulting roles.
  • Client Presentation: Presented findings and actionable recommendations to a mock client in a clear, structured manner.

🛠 Tools and Technologies Used 🛠

  • PowerPoint: Used for creating professional presentations and delivering findings to stakeholders.
  • Excel: Analyzed and processed data to support business recommendations.
  • SQL: Managed and queried data to extract insights for decision-making.
